Lithium Wars Online

A chaotic 2d open-world multiplayer pvp platformer game that you can play right in your browser! Over time, you will find Lithium chunks and weapons across the map that can increase your power. Once you are strong enough, you can fight other players and compete for the leaderboard. But if you die, you start over with nothing. So the question is: how long will you last?
(currently in development).
